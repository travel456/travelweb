Magical Nepal

Kathmandu • Chitwan • Pokhara (6 Nights / 7 Days)

Trip Overview

Experience the best of Nepal with this comprehensive 7-day tour. From the spiritual temples of Kathmandu to the wildlife of Chitwan and the majestic mountain views of Pokhara. Includes private sedan transfer and jungle activities.

Day-wise Itinerary

1

Arrival in Kathmandu

Arrive in Kathmandu - upon arrival our airport representative will meet, assist and transfer to Hotel. Welcome drink at hotel and proceed for briefing regarding the program. At evening visit Bouddha Stupa and Pashupatinath Temple. Overnight at hotel in Kathmandu.

  • Bouddhanath Stupa: One of the largest stupas in the world, a hub of Tibetan Buddhism adorned with colorful prayer flags.
  • Pashupatinath Temple: A revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, located along the banks of the sacred Bagmati River.
2

Kathmandu to Chitwan Drive

After breakfast and early morning activities drive to Chitwan [Approx 180 km]. En-route visit to Manakamana Temple. Evening attend Tharu Cultural Dance show. Overnight stay at Hotel in Chitwan.

Manakamana Temple: Accessible by cable car, this sacred shrine is dedicated to Goddess Bhagwati and offers stunning views of the Trishuli River.

3

Chitwan Safari Tour

Early in the morning after breakfast visit for Canoe and Elephant Breeding Center. After lunch proceed for Jungle Safari tour in Elephant or Jeep at Chitwan National Park. Overnight stay at hotel in Chitwan.

Chitwan National Park: A UNESCO World Heritage Site renowned for its rich biodiversity, dense forests, and captivating wildlife.

4

Drive to Pokhara

Early in the morning after breakfast drive to Pokhara [Approx 160 km]. Overnight stay at hotel in Pokhara.

5

Pokhara Full-Day Sightseeing Tour

Early morning drive to Sarangkot (approx 13 km) to enjoy sunrise view with magnificent mountain ranges. En-route visit Bindabasani temple. Drive back to hotel and after breakfast proceed for half-day sightseeing tour. Overnight stay at Hotel in Pokhara.

  • Sarangkot: Famous for panoramic vistas of Annapurna and Dhaulagiri ranges.
  • Bindabasani Temple: A revered Hindu shrine dedicated to goddess Bindabasani.
  • Devi’s Falls: A mesmerizing waterfall that disappears underground.
  • Gupteshwor Cave: A mystical underground cave housing a sacred Shiva lingam.
  • Taal Barahi Temple: An ornate pagoda-style temple located on an island in Phewa Lake.
6

Pokhara to Kathmandu Drive

Early in the morning after breakfast, drive back to Kathmandu and transfer to Hotel [Approx 200 km]. Overnight stay at Hotel in Kathmandu.

7

Departure

Early in the morning after breakfast, transfer to the international airport for your onward departure flight.
