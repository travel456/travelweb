Statue of Unity

Kevadia, Gujarat (2 Nights / 3 Days)

Closed on Mondays

Trip Overview

Experience the world's tallest statue and the beautiful surroundings of Kevadia. This package includes the Viewing Gallery, Jungle Safari, Glow Garden, and a comfortable stay with meals.

Day-wise Itinerary

1

Arrival, Safari & SOU Visit

11:00 AM: Check-in to Resort.

11:00 AM - 02:00 PM: Jungle Safari.

02:00 PM - 04:00 PM: Free time & Lunch (E-rickshaw drop available, lunch on own).

04:00 PM: Visit Statue of Unity Viewing Gallery.

07:00 PM: Narmada Aarti.

07:45 PM: Laser Show (Subject to availability).

08:00 PM - 10:30 PM: Dinner at Resort.

2

Gardens & Eco Tour

07:30 AM - 09:30 AM: Morning Breakfast.

09:30 AM - 12:00 PM: Free time (Optional: River Rafting/Cycling at extra charge).

12:00 PM - 08:00 PM: Tour of Butterfly Garden, Cactus Garden, Vishwa Van, Ekta Nursery, Dino Trail.

Evening: Visit the beautiful Glow Garden.

3

Departure

Morning breakfast at the resort. Check-out at 10:00 AM with happy memories.
